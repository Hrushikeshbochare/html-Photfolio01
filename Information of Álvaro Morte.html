Álvaro Morte

Álvaro Antonio García Pérez (born 23 February 1975), known professionally as Álvaro Morte, is a Spanish actor. He gained worldwide recognition for playing the role of 'The Professor' in the television series Money Heist. Morte plays Logain Ablar, in the Amazon Prime Video's high fantasy series, The Wheel of Time.
